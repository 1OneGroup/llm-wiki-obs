# Marketing Strategy: The Clermont | LAUNCH

## Campaign Concept

- **big_idea:** "150 acres of township living meets aluminium-crafted precision — the neighbourhood you've been building your career for"
- **rationale:** Convergence of scale (150-acre township), construction integrity (aluminium formwork), location timing (adjacent GMADA urban forest). Rs 1.60 Cr buyers are apartment upgraders wanting independence without isolation. 216-unit community within mega township delivers that.
- **positioning:** For Tricity professionals seeking first independent home — premium 3BHK floors with township-scale living and apartment-grade precision
- **competitive_angle:** Rare intersection of ready independent floors within 150-acre ecosystem + aluminium formwork tech. Most Mohali offers either plots (DIY) or high-rises (shared walls).

---

## Tone

> [!warning] Content Guardrails for Clermont
> - **Do:** Lead with facts (150 acres, 216 units, 15 min airport) | Conversational confidence | Paint daily life not abstract luxury
> - **Don't:** No "dream home/world-class/bespoke" | No vague claims | No competitor name-drops

- **character:** Confident, grounded, specific. Warm but never over-promising.
- **sample:** "Your morning jog ends at a zen garden. Your kids cycle to school within the township. Your car charges while you sleep. This is what 150 acres of planning looks like."

- **age:** 35-42 | **income:** 25-40 LPA | **occupation:** IT mid-senior | **family:** married, 1-2 kids (3-10)

- **values:** Education, work-life balance, asset building, safety
- **pain_points:** Outgrown apartment, construction quality worry, distance from job hubs
- **media:** LinkedIn, YouTube home tours, Instagram, WhatsApp IT groups, 99acres
