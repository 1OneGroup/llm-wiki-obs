# ONE Group — Audience Content Map

> Maps each buyer persona to the right content angle, platform, and messaging hook. Use this when deciding what to write for a specific post.
>
> For project-specific persona details see [[projects/the-clermont]] and [[projects/the-saavira]].

---

## The Clermont Personas

### 1. Tricity Upgraders
**Who:** IT/tech professionals in Chandigarh-Mohali-Panchkula, 30–45, family-oriented, upgrading from apartment to independent floor.

| Platform | Content Angle | Hook |
|----------|--------------|------|
| Instagram | Lifestyle — green address, private floor, township life | "Your own floor. No shared walls. 15 min from the office." |
| Facebook | Family community — schools, safety, parks nearby | "Where do Tricity families actually want to live?" |
| LinkedIn | Career milestone angle — "you've earned the upgrade" | "After 10 years in IT, this is what the next chapter looks like." |
| YouTube | Site walkthrough + township tour | Virtual tour: "Why Sector 98 is the Tricity's next address" |

**What works:** Specific proximity data (15 min airport, schools nearby), MIVAN construction quality, township independence.
**What doesn't:** Price as the lead angle, comparisons to apartments they came from.

---

### 2. NRI Investors
**Who:** Indian diaspora in Canada, UK, US, UAE. 35–55. Looking for low-maintenance, trusted developer, RE appreciation.

| Platform | Content Angle | Hook |
|----------|--------------|------|
| Instagram | Investment returns + lifestyle for return visits | "Your parents are close. Your investment is safe. Your floor is ready." |
| Facebook | NRI community trust + developer credibility | "How to buy property in India without the headaches." |
| LinkedIn | ROI and yield content, developer track record | "Why NRIs choose debt-free developers in a leveraged market." |
| YouTube | Virtual site tour — they can't visit easily | "Full walkthrough of The Clermont — for NRIs considering Mohali" |

**What works:** Rs 0 debt (huge trust signal for NRIs burned by delayed projects), RERA registration, virtual tour availability, low-maintenance independent floor.
**What doesn't:** Urgency language, discounts, "call now" energy. They're comparing carefully.

> [!note] NRI Timing
> Post NRI-targeted content at 9–11pm IST to reach Canada morning / UK evening.

---

### 3. Chandigarh Business Owners
**Who:** Self-employed, established businesses, 40–60. Status-conscious but low-key. Buy in cash or minimal loan.

| Platform | Content Angle | Hook |
|----------|--------------|------|
| Instagram | Status + independence — private floor, not apartment | "An address that reflects what you've built." |
| Facebook | Community + local connection — Mohali growth story | "Why established Chandigarh families are moving to Sector 98." |
| LinkedIn | Investment rationale — developer's financial prudence mirrors theirs | "A developer with zero external debt. Just like good business practice." |
| YouTube | Township overview — the bigger picture | "150 acres, 216 families — not a complex, a community." |

**What works:** Independence angle (no shared lifts/walls = status), ONE Group's financial prudence, urban forest adjacency.
**What doesn't:** Tech-heavy construction details, investment yield talk — they know what they want.

## The Saavira Personas

### 4. C-Suite Upgraders
**Who:** VPs, CXOs at MNCs in Gurgaon. 38–55. Income Rs 1 Cr+/year. Upgrading from premium to truly exclusive.

| Platform | Content Angle | Hook |
|----------|--------------|------|
| Instagram | Exclusivity signal — 72 families, one tower, zero compromise | "72 residences. Not 720. That's the point." |
| Facebook | Skip or very minimal — this audience isn't on Facebook for luxury RE | N/A |
| LinkedIn | Peer positioning — others at this level live here | "Where Gurgaon's decision-makers call home." |
| YouTube | Model apartment walkthrough, architect interview | "Inside The Saavira — a G+25 tower with 72 families" |

**What works:** Scarcity (72 units only), Singapore-inspired design, G+25 with views, developer's zero-delay record.
**What doesn't:** Price-per-sqft comparisons, features lists, "good value" framing — they're buying exclusivity, not value.

---

### 5. Entrepreneur-Investors
**Who:** Founders, partners in professional services. 35–50. Buy RE as portfolio asset + lifestyle. Numbers-minded.

| Platform | Content Angle | Hook |
|----------|--------------|------|
| Instagram | Scarcity + investment thesis | "In a market of 500-unit towers, 72 units is the real differentiator." |
| Facebook | Investment community angle — why scarcity = premium | "Why luxury towers fail and how The Saavira avoids it." |
| LinkedIn | Portfolio diversification, developer's financial model | "The developers who borrowed nothing, built everything." |
| YouTube | Market commentary + Saavira case study | "Why ultra-luxury in Gurgaon works when done right" |

**What works:** Rs 0 debt developer angle (mirrors their own business discipline), 72-unit scarcity, Sector 48 location fundamentals.
**What doesn't:** Lifestyle-first content. They lead with logic, feel later.

---

### 6. Global Indians (Returned from Singapore/Dubai)
**Who:** Professionals returned from or considering return from Singapore, Dubai, Hong Kong. 35–50. Expect international-grade living.

| Platform | Content Angle | Hook |
|----------|--------------|------|
| Instagram | Singapore-parity design + Indian roots | "Singapore-style living. Gurgaon address. Indian soul." |
| Facebook | Community of returnees — familiar faces, familiar standards | "Coming back to India doesn't mean compromising." |
| LinkedIn | India's rising premium RE story + personal narrative | "Why I chose Gurgaon over Singapore — and what I found." |
| YouTube | Apartment tour with Singapore design comparison | "The Saavira vs Singapore condo — what you get in Gurgaon in 2026" |

**What works:** Singapore/international design references (they'll get it), G+25 tower form factor, high-quality finishes, one-tower exclusivity.
**What doesn't:** Indian-subcontinent comparisons ("better than Delhi projects") — frame against international, not local.

## Cross-Persona Posts

> [!note] Universal Trust Content
> Some content works across all segments — use it for your trust/corporate slot.

| Post Type | Why It Works Universally |
|-----------|-------------------------|
| "Rs 0 debt since 2002" | Every buyer segment values developer stability |
| "6,000+ families" | Social proof is universal |
| RERA registration + project details | Buyers across all segments verify this |
| Construction milestone updates | Reduces anxiety for all existing leads |
| Festival greetings | Cultural connection — no buyer segment is excluded |
