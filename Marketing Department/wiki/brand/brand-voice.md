## 4. Brand Voice & Tone

### Voice Guidelines

* **Confident but not boastful** — our track record speaks for itself
* **Warm and reassuring** — like a trusted advisor guiding you through a big decision
* **Grounded in experience** — we've built through every market cycle for 20+ years
* **Never desperate** — we don't need to sell; we invite you to buy
* **Forward-looking** — young leadership building on strong foundations

### Style Tips

* **Specifics over generics** — "3 BHK starting at Rs 1.60 Cr in 150-acre township" beats "luxury apartments available"
* **Hindi-English code-switching** is natural for north Indian markets — use it

### The Tone: Quiet Confidence

Substance over flash. Headlines whisper, never shout. No flashy sales events. No urgency tactics. No celebrity endorsements. No "limited time offers" or "book now" pressure.

### What One Group is NOT

* **Not flashy or loud** — no over-the-top launches
* **Not aggressive** — no pressure-driven sales
* **Not new to this** — 20+ years of trust behind us
* **Not vague** — specific commitments backed by delivered projects
* **Not old-school patriarchal** — young professionals, not distant elders

> [!warning] Banned Words
> **Never use in any content:** dream home, world-class, bespoke, ultra-luxury, exclusive offer, limited time, hurry, don't miss out

### Content Do's and Don'ts

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Lead with facts and proof points | Use vague claims or superlatives |
| Paint daily life, not abstract luxury | Say "dream home / world-class / bespoke" |
| Show construction progress | Name-drop competitors |
| Use conversational confidence | Use pressure tactics or urgency |
| Include specific numbers | Promise what isn't verified in project docs |

## 11. Brand Architecture

### Model: Endorsed Brand

**Formula:** **[Unique Project Name] by One Group**

### Sub-Brands

| Sub-Brand                 | Segment             | Positioning Line                       |
| ------------------------- | ------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| **Saavira by One Group**  | Premium Residential | *Elevated living, grounded values*     |
| **One City by One Group** | Township Projects   | *Complete communities, complete trust* |

> [!warning] Naming Rules
> - Create unique, distinctive name reflecting the project's character
> - Avoid the "One" prefix for new projects (legacy convention)
> - Avoid competitor-sounding names
> - Always add "by One Group" endorsement
> - Legacy projects (One City, One Rise, One Mall, One Farms, One Square) remain as-is — sold out and delivered
