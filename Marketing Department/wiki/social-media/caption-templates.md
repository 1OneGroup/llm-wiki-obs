# ONE Group — Caption Templates

> **Purpose:** Ready-to-use caption formulas with filled examples, CTA guidance, carousel anatomy, and the full hashtag bank.
> Each template follows: **Hook (under 125 chars) > Value (2-4 lines) > CTA > 3-5 hashtags**.
>
> **Related pages:**
> - [[social-media/platform-rules]] — Platform-specific rules, posting times, format priorities
> - [[social-media/content-strategy]] — Content pillars, persona mapping, seasonal windows

---

## Caption Length Guidelines by Platform

| Platform | Length | Key Rule |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Instagram | 150-250 words | Keyword-rich first 2 lines. Hook before ~125-char truncation. |
| Facebook | 150-300 words | Conversational. Ask a question in every caption. Shorten to 150w for promo posts. |
| LinkedIn | 150-300 words (or 500-1500 chars text-only) | Open with data point or counterintuitive insight. Generous line breaks. |
| YouTube description | 300+ words | Hook + link before fold. Keywords below. |

## Carousel Anatomy / Slide Structure

Carousels earn saves — the algorithm's strongest signal on Instagram and LinkedIn.

### Instagram Carousel (5-7 slides)

| Slide | Content | Purpose |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 1 | Scroll-stopping hook image + bold headline | Stop the scroll. This is the ad for the rest of the carousel. |
| 2-3 | Core value / differentiators / data points | Deliver the promise of slide 1 |
| 4-5 | Supporting proof or detail | Build depth and credibility |
| 6 (optional) | Summary or key takeaway | Reinforces the message |
| Last | CTA slide | "DM us [keyword]", "Save this", "Follow for more" |

### LinkedIn Document (PDF carousel, 5-8 slides)

| Slide | Content |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | Bold question or surprising stat — stop the scroll |
| 2-4 | Data, analysis, insight — one point per slide |
| 5-7 | Supporting evidence, comparisons |
| Last | Discussion prompt + company mention |

> [!note] Design Rules for Carousels
> - One idea per slide
> - Large text, minimal clutter
> - Brand colours: `#6B1C23` `#F9F6F1` `#C9A962`
> - First slide must work as a standalone image in the feed
