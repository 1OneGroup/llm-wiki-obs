## 2026 Algorithm Rules by Platform

> [!note] Discovery in 2026
> Discovery now happens through keyword-rich captions, not hashtags. Engagement quality (comments, saves, shares) matters more than volume (likes).

### Instagram

- **Format priority:** Reels > Carousel > Single image
- **Reels:** 15-30 seconds for feed engagement; up to 90 seconds for educational content. Use trending audio only when relevant to brand.
- **Carousel:** 5-7 slides. First slide = scroll-stopping hook + bold headline. Last slide = CTA. Carousels earn saves — the algorithm's strongest signal.
- **Caption:** 150-250 words. **Keyword-rich first 2 lines** — Instagram indexes caption text for search and Explore placement. Hook must land before the ~125-character truncation point. One idea per paragraph. Line breaks for readability.
- **Hashtags:** **3-5 max** (2026 rule). Place at very end of caption. See hashtag strategy below.
- **Stories:** 3-4/week minimum. Use polls, questions, "tap for details" stickers, and countdown stickers for launches/events. Stories do NOT require board approval.
- **Engagement window:** Reply to every comment within the first 30-60 minutes. This signals real conversation and boosts distribution.
- **CTA:** Every post ends with one of: "Link in bio", "DM us [keyword]", "Save this post", "Tag someone who needs to see this".

**Algorithm signal ranking (2026):**
1. **Shares (DM forwards)** — most weighted. Design content people send to family/friends.
2. **Saves** — second most weighted. Educational carousels and checklists drive saves.
3. **Comments** — ask questions in captions, use polls in stories.
4. **Watch time on Reels** — hook in first 2 seconds, text overlay for silent viewers.

### Facebook

- **Format priority:** Short video (under 60 sec) > Single image. Carousels underperform video.
- **Caption:** 150-300 words. Conversational, community-focused. **Ask a question in every caption** — Meta's Andromeda algorithm rewards comment threads.
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 only. Location + project name. Hashtags serve as filing labels, not growth levers.
- **Dark Social:** DMs and group conversations drive more conversions than public feed posts. Participate in local Tricity and Gurgaon property groups. Share content that gets forwarded in private (buyer guides, price comparisons).
- **Stories:** 2/week. Mirror top Instagram stories.
- **CTA:** "Comment below", "Share with someone looking for a home in [city]", "Drop your question".
- **Strategy:** Repost top Instagram content with adapted captions (longer, more conversational). Join NRI community groups. Use Facebook Events for site visits and launches. Use Marketplace for property listings (free, high intent).

### LinkedIn

- **Format priority:** Native document (PDF carousel) > Text + single image > Native video. Documents get 3x the reach of text posts.
- **Caption:** 150-300 words. Professional, investment and achievement angle. **Open with a surprising data point or counterintuitive insight.**
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 only. Industry + location. Never use generic brand tags — LinkedIn penalises irrelevant tagging.
- **Founder-first strategy:** Personal profiles generate 8x more engagement than company pages. Post from leadership profiles (CMD, CMO) when possible. Employee reshares reach 561% further than company page posts.
- **No external links in post body** — LinkedIn deprioritises posts with outbound links. Put links in the FIRST COMMENT. Reference with "Link in comments".
- **Meaningful comments:** The algorithm weights a single 10-word substantive comment far more than 10 likes. End with a genuine discussion prompt, not a generic "Thoughts?"
- **Frequency:** 2-3 posts/week on company page. 3-4/week on founder profile.
- **CTA:** "What's your read on this?", "DM me for the full breakdown", "Share if this resonates".
- Tag 1-2 relevant people max per post (over-tagging is penalised).

> [!warning] What NOT to Post on LinkedIn
> - Festival wishes (feels out of place)
> - Render-heavy promotional content without substance
> - Discount or scheme announcements
> - Generic motivational content

### YouTube

- **Shorts (cross-post):** 15-60 seconds. Construction walks, location aerials, lifestyle moments. Cross-post from Instagram Reels with minor adaptation.
- **Long-form:** 3-8 minutes (walkthroughs, market commentary, buyer education guides, architect interviews). Up to 15 min for detailed project tours.
- **Title:** Benefit-first + location keyword for SEO. E.g., "Why Sector 98 Mohali is the Tricity's next premium address" — not "Clermont Update #4". Include city + config + type + developer name.
- **Description:** First 2 lines visible before fold — hook + link to project page. Below fold: full project details, timestamps, keywords. 300+ words of keyword-rich text.
- **Timestamps (chapters):** Include in every video — improves watch time metrics significantly.
- **Thumbnails:** High-contrast, one face or one striking visual, 3-5 words max. No clutter. Show the actual property.
- **Pinned comment:** WhatsApp number + "DM us for brochure" CTA.
- **Tags:** 10-15 relevant tags including city, project name, configuration, competitor project names.
- **Consistency:** A well-lit phone walkthrough posted weekly outperforms a cinematic video posted monthly. The algorithm rewards weekly uploads.

**Shorts strategy:** Shorts feed into long-form. Viewers who discover you through a 30-second clip get recommended your full walkthrough. Use Shorts as top-of-funnel.

## Hashtag Strategy (2026 Rule)

> [!note] Hashtags Are Filing Labels, Not Growth Levers
> Instagram killed hashtag following in 2026. Discovery weight has shifted to keywords in captions. Write keyword-rich captions first, then add 3-5 curated hashtags as category signals.

### The Rule: 1 Brand + 2-4 Contextual

Every post gets **one brand hashtag** plus **2-4 contextual tags** from the relevant set.

| Platform | Count | Placement |
|----------|-------|-----------|
| Instagram | 3-5 | End of caption |
| Facebook | 3-5 | End of caption |
| LinkedIn | 3-5 | End of post |
| YouTube | 10-15 | Video tags + description |

**Brand hashtags (pick one per post):**
- `#OneGroupDevelopers` — default
- `#TheOneYouCanTrust` — for trust/corporate posts
- `#DebtFreeSince2002` — for credibility-heavy posts

For the full hashtag bank organized by category, see [[social-media/caption-templates]].

### Keyword-First Discovery

The real reach driver in 2026 is **natural language in your caption**. Weave these phrases into the body text:

- **Location:** "independent floors in Mohali", "luxury apartments in Gurgaon Sector 48", "Sohna Road premium residences"
- **Features:** "3BHK independent floor", "G+25 tower with 72 units", "MIVAN construction technology"
- **Buyer intent:** "best real estate investment in Tricity", "NRI property buying guide India", "RERA approved project Mohali"
- **Trust:** "debt-free developer India", "zero delayed projects", "6000 families trust ONE Group"

## Engagement Windows and Response Times

### The 60-Minute Rule

After every post, engage actively with EVERY comment for the first 60 minutes. This dramatically boosts algorithmic reach.

- Reply to every comment with a genuine, specific response (not "Thank you!")
- Ask a follow-up question in your reply to keep the thread going
- Heart/like every comment
- If someone asks a project question in comments, respond publicly AND DM them privately

### Response Time SLAs

| Channel | Response Time |
|---------|--------------|
| Instagram DMs | Within 5 minutes during business hours |
| Comments (first 60 min) | Reply to every single one |
| WhatsApp inquiries | First reply within 5 minutes (business hours), 30 minutes outside |
| LinkedIn comments | Within 60 minutes of posting |

### WhatsApp Handoff Protocol

Every social media inquiry should reach WhatsApp within 5 minutes. WhatsApp is THE conversion platform in Indian RE.

**Flow:** Social media post > DM inquiry > WhatsApp handoff > Site visit booking

- Pin WhatsApp numbers in Instagram bio link
- Include WhatsApp link in every YouTube video description and pinned comment
- Use WhatsApp Business catalog for floor plans, pricing, and brochures
- Post construction updates and site clips to WhatsApp Status for warm leads

## Organic Reach Tactics

No paid budget means organic reach tactics are non-negotiable.

### Design for "Send to Friend" Behaviour

Shares (DM forwards) are the #1 algorithm signal. Create content people instinctively forward:
- "Tag your friend who's house-hunting in Mohali"
- Surprising data: "Did you know Sector 98 is closer to the airport than Sector 22?"
- Relatable concerns: "POV: You're comparing 5 builders and they all claim to be 'trusted'"
- Checklists that are genuinely useful (not disguised ads)

### Instagram Collab Posts

Use Instagram's Collab feature with local interior designers, lifestyle bloggers, RE educators, channel partners, and happy customers. Each collab instantly exposes content to their entire audience — free.

### Engagement Pods (Ethical Version)

Build a WhatsApp group of 15-20 team members, channel partners, and loyal customers. When you post, share it in the group and ask for genuine engagement in the first 30 minutes.

## Tone Guardrails

> [!warning] Always / Never Rules
> **Always:**
> - Quiet confidence. Substance over flash.
> - Specific details (floor count, RERA numbers, sq ft) over vague claims
> - "6,000+ families trust ONE Group" — use the number, it is real
> - Short sentences. One idea per sentence.
>
> **Never:**
> - Dream home, world-class, bespoke, ultra-luxury, limited time, hurry
> - Exclamation marks (more than once per post)
> - ALL CAPS for emphasis
> - Fabricating details — if unsure, leave it out
> - Emojis as decoration (only use if they replace a word or add meaning)

## Content Workflow

1. **Monthly calendar:** CMO prepares using pillar ratios and Indian RE calendar (see [[social-media/content-strategy]])
2. **Weekly updates:** Calendar updated based on trending topics and platform performance
3. **Board approval:** Calendar submitted for board review
4. **Content creation:** AI agent generates images (Gemini/Nano Banana) and writes copy using [[social-media/caption-templates]]
5. **Draft to Postiz:** Save approved image + content as drafts in Postiz
6. **Schedule/Publish:** Board moves drafts to scheduled, or posts immediately via MCP
7. **Engage:** 60-minute active engagement after every post. Respond to every DM within 5 minutes.

Stories do not require board approval. Post freely 3-5x per day.
