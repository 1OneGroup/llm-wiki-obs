# Post Design Templates — One Group Developers

> **Purpose:** Reference guide for the graphic-designer AI agent. Describes 10 post design categories with layout, copy formula, color/mood, and visual differentiators so the agent knows which style to generate for any given post request.
> **Brand reference:** Read [[brand/brand-voice]] for full brand guidelines before generating any post.
> **Reference images location:** `_context/graphic_design_manager/post/` — all 28 reference images are stored here.

## Shared Branding DNA (applies to all categories unless noted)

| Element | Specification |
|---------|--------------|
| **Primary Color** | Deep Maroon `#6B1C23` |
| **Secondary Color** | Warm Ivory `#F9F6F1` |
| **Accent Color** | Aged Gold `#C9A962` |
| **Header Font** | Georgia (serif) |
| **Body Font** | Arial (sans-serif) |
| **Logo** | Red "ONE" wordmark with gold accents |
| **Aesthetic** | Quiet luxury — premium but not flashy |

> [!warning] Banned Words (All Design Categories)
> Never use in any post copy: dream home, world-class, bespoke, ultra-luxury, exclusive offer, limited time, hurry, don't miss out

## 1. Festival / Occasion Greetings

**When to Use:** Indian festivals, national days, religious occasions, cultural celebrations.

**Format:** Portrait (1080x1920)

**Layout Zones:**
- Top: One Group logo, center-aligned
- Middle: Large decorative/script headline with festival greeting. Festive or culturally relevant central image (idol, portrait, family photo, illustration)
- Bottom: Corporate footer strip — "GROUP HOUSINGS | TOWNSHIPS | COMMERCIAL"
- Some posts include chairman name (Sunil Kumar Jain) above the footer

**Typography Style:** Decorative serif or script for the festival name (large, expressive). Georgia serif for subtext greeting. Mix of Hindi and English is natural.

**Color/Mood:** Warm, festive tones. Gold accents. Rich reds, oranges, deep greens depending on the festival. Warm Ivory backgrounds. Soft ambient lighting in imagery.

**Copy Formula:**
- `"Happy [Festival Name]"` — simple greeting
- `"[Emotive phrase] — Happy [Festival Name]"` — e.g., "Celebrate the birth of the divine — Happy Janmashtami"
- `"[Aspirational message tied to festival values]"` — e.g., "Celebrating Peace & Truth — Happy Gandhi Jayanti"

**Festival Tier System:**

| Tier | Examples | Posts | CTA Allowed? |
|------|----------|-------|-------------|
| RE Peak festival | Diwali, Akshaya Tritiya, Dhanteras, Baisakhi | 3 | Soft CTA OK (e.g., "Invest in your future") |
| Major festival | Holi, Navratri start, Dussehra, Guru Nanak Jayanti | 2 | No CTA — brand/emotional only |
| Minor festival | Guru Purnima, Nag Panchami, Buddha Purnima | 1 | No CTA |
| National day | Republic Day, Independence Day, Gandhi Jayanti | 1 | No CTA |

> [!warning] No-Go Periods (Override ALL Categories)
> - **Adhik Maas** (May 17 – Jun 15, 2026): No property purchase CTAs, no Griha Pravesh messaging
> - **Pitru Paksha** (Sep 27 – Oct 10, 2026): No "new venture" or "new beginning" messaging
> - **Monsoon** (Jul–Aug): Brand/community content only, no site-visit CTAs

## 2. Project Launch / Teaser

**When to Use:** New project announcements, pre-launch reveals, "coming soon" teasers, RERA approval announcements.

**Format:** Portrait (1080x1920)

**Layout Zones:**
- Top: One Group logo, center-aligned
- Middle: Bold aspirational headline + price highlight in a contrasting badge/banner (`3 BHK | ₹X.XX Cr* Onwards`). Dramatic reveal imagery (red curtain, flowing shapes, building silhouette)
- Bottom: "Launching Soon" or "Coming Soon" CTA. Location pin with project address.

**Typography Style:** Bold sans-serif or mixed serif/sans for headline. Price in large, high-contrast text. Gold or white on dark background.

**Color/Mood:** Dramatic and premium — deep royal blue, gold accents, dark backgrounds with spotlight effects. Sense of anticipation and exclusivity. Red/gold for Hindi-language variants.

**Copy Formula:**
- `"[Aspirational tagline] — [Config] ₹[Price] Onwards — Launching Soon at [Location]"`
- Hindi variant: `"[Hindi tagline] — [Project Name] — [Location] — का शुभारंभ शीघ्र"`

**Reference Files:**
- `LAUNCH EDM.jpg` — Clermont launch teaser (blue/gold, red curtain reveal)
- `1920 rohtak phase 2 rera.jpg` — One City Rohtak RERA announcement (Hindi, elegant neutrals)

## 3. Project Sales / Print Ad

**When to Use:** Newspaper ads, detailed project promotion with full specs, price-focused sales posts, RERA-compliant advertisements.

**Format:** Variable — portrait (1080x1920) for print ads, square (1080x1080) for digital.

**Layout Zones:**
- Top: Project logo/name (The Clermont, One City) + RERA number + one group logo
- Middle: Building render or site photograph. Bold headline with config + price. USP badges arranged horizontally or vertically ("Debt-Free Developers", "100% Paid-Up Land Bank", "Construction Under Full Swing")
- Bottom: Phone number CTA ("Call for More Details ☎ [Number]"). Full address with regional/head office details.

**Typography Style:** Mixed — serif for project name, bold sans for price/CTA. Dense text layout. Information hierarchy: Project Name > Price > Location > USPs > Contact.

**Color/Mood:** Clean and informational. Blue sky with building render for aspirational feel. White/cream backgrounds for print. Can be bilingual (Hindi/English).

**Copy Formula:**
- `"[Project Name] — [Config] Apartments — [Location] — Price Starting From ₹[X] Cr* — [USP badges] — Call [Phone Number]"`

**Reference Files:**
- `25 dec One Group Newspaper Ad Final curve.jpg` — The Clermont full newspaper ad
- `1200x1200.jpg` — One City Rohtak square digital ad with site photo

## 4. Location Connectivity

**When to Use:** Showcasing a project's proximity to key landmarks — hospitals, airports, schools, stadiums, business hubs. Best as a multi-slide carousel.

**Format:** Square (1080x1080)

**Layout Zones:**
- Intro slide: Project logo (The Clermont) top-center + RERA number top-right. Aspirational location tagline in large text. Building render as background. One Group logo top-left.
- Subsequent slides: Full-bleed real photograph of the landmark. Bold landmark name in white/gold. `"in [X] MINS"` with the number stylized extra-large in Gold. Swipe indicator arrow bottom-right. One Group logo top-left.

**Typography Style:** Bold sans-serif for landmark names. Numbers (minutes) in extra-large stylized Gold. White text on photo with subtle shadow for readability.

**Color/Mood:** Photo-led with warm overlay. Night/golden hour photography for landmarks. Gold and white text. Each slide is its own mini-billboard.

**Copy Formula:**
- Intro slide: `"[Project Name] — [Aspirational location tagline]"` — e.g., "The Clermont — A Lifestyle at the Center of It All"
- Landmark slides: `"[Landmark Name] in [X] MINS"` — e.g., "Chandigarh International Airport in 20 MINS"

## 5. Amenity Showcase

**When to Use:** Highlighting individual amenities of a project — gym, jogging track, kids' area, garden, sports courts, gazebo, nature spaces.

**Format:** Portrait (1080x1920) for standalone posts. Square (1080x1080) when used in a carousel. Default to portrait for standalone.

**Layout Zones:**
- Top: Project logo (The Clermont) center-aligned + RERA number top-right corner + website URL top-left
- Middle: Large emotive/aspirational headline in decorative serif. Lifestyle photography of people using the amenity or nature scene.
- Lower-middle: Amenity label badge — bordered box with small icon + amenity name (e.g., "Open Gym", "Kids' Play Area", "Basketball Court")
- Bottom: Location pin with project address (e.g., "One City Hamlet-4, Sector 98, Mohali")

**Typography Style:** Large decorative Georgia serif for emotive headline. Cursive/script for accent words. Arial for amenity badge text. Mix of sizes creates visual hierarchy.

**Color/Mood:** Varies by amenity — warm golden hour for outdoor amenities, rich green for nature/garden, moody dark tones for evening activities (basketball, gazebo). Always lifestyle-forward — show the experience, not just the space.

**Copy Formula:**
- `"[Emotive headline about the experience]"` + `"[Amenity Name badge]"`
- Examples:
  - "Wellness under the Sky — Open Gym"
  - "Where Joyful Memories Begin — Kids' Play Area"
  - "Game On Anytime — Basketball Court"
  - "Your Peaceful Escape — Gazebo"
  - "Home in Nature, Nature in Home"

## 6. Brand Lifestyle / USP

**When to Use:** Posts that highlight project-level USPs — facing details, road width, location advantages, luxury positioning. Use when the focus is on the property's unique selling proposition rather than a specific amenity or festival.

**Format:** Portrait (1080x1920) or square (1080x1080)

**Layout Zones:**
- Top: Project logo (The Clermont) center-aligned + RERA number + website URL
- Middle: Building render or lifestyle image. Bold headline highlighting the key USP.
- Lower-middle: Supporting detail badges in a horizontal strip (e.g., "On 26 Mt Wide Road | Park Facing | North Facing | East Facing")
- Bottom: Location pin with project address

**Typography Style:** Large decorative serif for USP headline (e.g., "Luxury Residences"). "All Units Preferentially Located" as supporting subtext. Badge text in clean sans-serif with small icons.

**Color/Mood:** Royal blue with gold accents for premium positioning. Building render with blue sky. Clean, authoritative, aspirational.

**Copy Formula:**
- `"[USP Headline] — [Supporting detail badges]"`
- Example: "Luxury Residences — All Units Preferentially Located — On 26 Mt Wide Road | Park Facing | North Facing | East Facing"

**Note:** This is a thin category with only one reference post. Use it for posts that highlight project-level USPs. As more examples are created, update the reference list.

**Reference Files:**

## 7. Educational / Informational

**When to Use:** FY-end tax benefits, Union Budget RE impact analysis, market trend explainers, investment tips, home-buying guides, Section 80C/24b breakdowns. Any post where the goal is to educate the audience, not promote a specific project.

**Format:** Square carousel (1080x1080), 5-7 slides. Carousels get 3x more engagement than single images.

**Layout Zones:**
- Top: One Group logo (left-aligned). **Maroon `#6B1C23` accent bar across the top edge** — this is the visual differentiator that signals "informational content"
- Slide 1: Typography-only — bold stat or question on Warm Ivory `#F9F6F1` background. No hero imagery. Let data lead.
- Inner slides (2-5): Maroon `#6B1C23` left sidebar with numbered points. Key stats/numbers highlighted in Gold `#C9A962`. Ivory background.
- Final slide: Soft CTA ("Follow for more insights" or "Save this for later") + One Group trust badge ("Debt-Free Since 2002 | The One You Can Trust")

**Typography Style:** Georgia serif for headline stat/question (large). Arial for body points. Numbers in extra-large Gold accent to draw the eye.

**Color/Mood:** Ivory background, Maroon text, Gold accents. Clean, authoritative, newspaper-editorial feel. No lifestyle imagery on slide 1 — this is data-led content.

**Copy Formula:**
- Slide 1: `"[Bold Stat or Question]"` — e.g., "5 Tax Benefits Every Homebuyer Should Claim Before March 31"
- Slides 2-5: `"[Numbered insight with supporting detail]"` — e.g., "1. Section 80C — Up to ₹1.5 lakh deduction on principal repayment"
- Final slide: `"Follow @one_group_developers for more | The One You Can Trust"`

> [!note] Educational Category Differentiators
> What carries from existing brand: Colors, logo, Georgia serif, "quiet confidence" authoritative tone.
> What's different: Maroon top bar (no other category has this), no hero image on slide 1, carousel-first format, data-led. Immediately distinguishable from festival greetings or project posts.

**Reference Files:** None yet — this is a new category. Follow the specifications above.

## 8. Trending / Pop Culture Tie-in

**When to Use:** Viral moments, movie releases (e.g., Dhurandhar), IPL cricket events, trending memes — adapted to a real estate message within 24 hours of the trend. Speed matters more than polish. The goal is shareability and cultural relevance.

**Format:** Square (1080x1080). Single image — not carousel (speed is critical for trend-jacking).

**Layout Zones:**
- Top: No logo header — the trending visual/meme takes full center stage
- Middle: Trending visual adapted to real estate message. Brand colors as accent only, not dominant. The trend leads, the brand follows.
- Bottom-right: One Group logo as **small watermark** (not header-sized)
- Border: **Gold `#C9A962` gradient border or corner accent** — the visual differentiator signaling "this is fun/current content"

**Typography Style:** Flexible — match the trending format's style. Can break Georgia/Arial convention to match the meme aesthetic. Bold, punchy, shareable.

**Color/Mood:** Led by the trend — One Group colors appear as accent only. No maroon/ivory dominance. Playful, current, shareable. The Gold border is the only consistent brand element.

**Copy Formula:**
- Image: `"[Pop culture reference / trending phrase adapted to real estate]"`
- Caption does the brand-linking work: `"[Witty real estate pivot] — One Group | #TrendingHashtag"`
- Example: A Dhurandhar-style post adapting "Bachcha hai tu mera" to homebuying: the meme visual leads, the real estate message is the punchline.

> [!warning] Trending Content Guard Rails
> - Must remain brand-safe — no controversy, no politics beyond national days
> - No competitor mentions
> - Never use banned words
> - Humor does not mean desperation — no "hurry", "limited time", or pressure tactics
> - Skip a trend if it can't be adapted brand-safely

**Reference Files:** None yet — this is a reactive category. Each post is unique to the trending moment.

## 9. Construction Update / Behind-the-Scenes

**When to Use:** Monthly/quarterly construction updates, monsoon-season brand content (Jul–Aug when marketing is minimal), milestone moments (foundation laid, structure complete, possession approaching). Brand message: "While others announce, we build."

**Format:** Portrait (1080x1920) for vertical drone shots. Square (1080x1080) for before/after split frames.

**Layout Zones:**
- Top: Project logo (The Clermont / The Saavira) — not One Group corporate logo. This is project-specific content.
- Top-right: **Gold `#C9A962` date stamp overlay** — e.g., "MARCH 2026 UPDATE" — this is the visual differentiator
- Middle: **Real photography ONLY** — drone shots, on-site progress photos, workers at work. No renders. No stock images. Authenticity is the point.
- Bottom: **Dark semi-transparent overlay strip** with progress milestone bar:
  `Foundation ✓ → Structure ✓ → Finishing → Possession`
  (check marks for completed stages, arrow pointing to current stage)
- Bottom strip: Trust badge bar — "Zero Delayed Projects | Debt-Free Since 2002"

**Typography Style:** Minimal text overlay — let construction speak. Date stamp in Gold on dark tag. Milestone bar in Warm Ivory on dark overlay. Arial sans-serif throughout.

**Color/Mood:** Real-world colors from site photography dominate. Overlays use Maroon/Gold on semi-transparent dark strips. Authentic, transparent, proof-driven. No glamour filters.

**Copy Formula:**
- Image overlay: `"[MONTH YEAR] UPDATE"` (date stamp only)
- Caption: `"[Project Name] — [Specific progress detail]. Your home is taking shape. Zero Delayed Projects | Debt-Free Since 2002"`
- Example: "The Clermont — Tower A structure complete, Tower B at 8th floor. Your home is taking shape."

> [!warning] Construction Update Rule
> Real photos ONLY. No renders, no stock images, no generated/AI images. This category cannot use anything other than authentic site photography.

**Reference Files:** None yet — use real site photography.

## 10. Infrastructure News Tie-in

**When to Use:** New infrastructure announcements near project locations — Infosys Mohali expansion (350K sq ft, ₹290 Cr, 3,000 jobs), Aerotropolis GMADA 6,000-acre development, Airport Road corridor expansion, metro extensions, hospital/school openings. Reactive to news events.

**Format:** Square (1080x1080). Can extend to 2-3 slide carousel for detailed announcements.

**Layout Zones:**
- Top: **"INFRA UPDATE" news ticker bar** — Maroon `#6B1C23` text on Gold `#C9A962` background strip — this is the visual differentiator (breaking-news banner aesthetic)
- Middle: Real photo or official render of the infrastructure (campus, highway, metro station). Bold headline with impact stat highlighted in large Gold text.
- Bottom-left: Distance callout badge: `"[X] mins from [Project Name]"` — reuses the proven format from Category 4 (Location Connectivity)
- Bottom-right: Source attribution: `"Source: [GMADA / ET Realty / Official Announcement]"`
- Bottom strip: One Group or project logo + location pin

**Typography Style:** Georgia serif for news headline. Bold Gold for key stat/number (extra-large). Arial for source attribution (small, italicized). Clean news-editorial hierarchy.

**Color/Mood:** News-editorial feel. The Maroon-on-Gold "INFRA UPDATE" bar is the signature element. Authoritative, opportunity-focused. The message is: "This development benefits your investment."

**Copy Formula:**
- `"INFRA UPDATE — [Infrastructure headline] — [Impact stat in Gold] — [X] mins from [Project Name]"`
- Source line: `"Source: [Attribution]"`
- Example: "INFRA UPDATE — Infosys Mohali Expansion: 350K sq ft, 3,000 new jobs — 15 mins from The Clermont — Source: GMADA"

> [!note] Category 10 vs Category 4 Difference
> Category 4 (Location Connectivity) is a static carousel showing known landmarks. Category 10 is **reactive to news events** — it has the news ticker bar, source attribution, and impact stat. It answers "what just happened and why it matters for your investment."

**Reference Files:** None yet — this is a reactive category. Use real photos of the infrastructure or official renders.

## Quick Reference: Category Identification Guide

Use this table when a post request comes in and you need to decide which template to use:

| If the request is about... | Use Category |
|---------------------------|-------------|
| Festival, national day, religious occasion | 1. Festival/Occasion Greetings |
| New project announcement, "coming soon", pre-launch | 2. Project Launch/Teaser |
| Full project details for print/digital ad with pricing + contact | 3. Project Sales/Print Ad |
| Showing how close a project is to key landmarks | 4. Location Connectivity |
| Highlighting a specific amenity (gym, park, pool, garden) | 5. Amenity Showcase |
| Project-level USP (facing, road width, luxury positioning) | 6. Brand Lifestyle/USP |
| Tax tips, budget analysis, market trends, investment education | 7. Educational/Informational |
| Viral trend, meme, movie/cricket tie-in | 8. Trending/Pop Culture |
| Construction progress, site photos, drone updates | 9. Construction Update/BTS |
| New infrastructure near project (metro, highway, IT park) | 10. Infrastructure News |
| Doesn't match any of the above | Ask the user which style to use |
